Ethno-Style in the interior

Ethno Style

In the ethno interior, the features of antiquity are transmitted as authentically as possible, when people did not yet know about modern materials. Therefore, preference should be given to natural wood and textiles, stone, metal. In this interior style, the decoration acts only as a backdrop for the main decorations - furniture, accessories.

Decor

But the mood of the whole interior is set, of course, by the decor in ethnic style. Arabic carpet, skins, African masks, Japanese vases, Chinese boxes, paintings, woven fabrics - these and other accessories perfectly convey the spirit and color of the people.

How did it start

Seafarers and travelers were the first to transfer the color of another nation to their homes. They brought memorabilia and souvenirs from long trips, which became part of the design of the home.

Requirements

There are no strict requirements for the design of ethnicity, the main thing is the choice in favor of natural materials and maintaining harmony between large and small decor - only in this way you can create an atmospheric room for rest or work.

Furniture

One of the most characteristic elements of ethno-style is furniture. Most items must be made of wood (bamboo, oak, beech, walnut, alder, pine, cedar). Upholstered furniture may have a metal frame and upholstery made of coarse natural fabrics. Furniture has a certain style, character and expressiveness. For example, wicker furniture belongs to the East and Africa. These are armchairs, chairs, ottomans, coffee and coffee tables.

In Ethno Style

The color scheme is unique for each direction in ethnic style. For example, the Chinese interior is traditionally decorated in red and gold, in Egyptian you can observe the dominance of warm shades - sand, yellow, brown, and in Scandinavian - colder restrained colors.
